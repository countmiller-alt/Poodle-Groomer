About Joe & Duke

Joe runs Duke’s out of a small, calm space in Tulsa — not a big-box chain — so your dog gets actual one-on-one attention from the person doing the grooming. He’s a family man with two daughters and a real soft spot for the dogs in his chair.

The shop’s namesake? Joe’s own poodle, Duke. Head mascot, head greeter, and (he insists) head haircut inspiration.

No assembly line. No rushing through six pups in an hour. Just careful work, a calm space, and a clean dog that comes home a little prouder than they left.
